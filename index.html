INSPIRATIONS

Here is some extra useless info about my interests and sources of inspiration
(Do not read!)

GAMES

Video games are a work of art. They allow us to explore stunning worlds and experience great stories that are shaped by our own actions. Video games aren't just entertainment, they're a gateway to boundless creativity. I love playing, analysing and reviewing video games. They're also a great source of inspiration for me. One of my goals is to recreate the old games' characters and environments and extend their story with my imagination as a fan art.

TECH & HARDWARE

I admit my only interest in computers was playing games on them when I was a kid. but one day I was amazed by the fact that how some hardware put together in a box could do such amazing things other than just playing amazing games. So I messed around with it and tried to know more than what average person knows and does with a pc. many years later I decided to learn coding (I regret it) and now I'm making this website from scratch like an idiot.

MUSIC

We never forget all these great movies and videogames that are packed with great story-telling and incredible visuals. But did you know that what actually makes them more memorable is their unique music and soundtracks? It doesn't matter if it's just an ambient soundtrack like in skyrim or a hyping music like in DOOM (yeah all those letters must be in uppercase) or a great boss music in dark souls. They all do one thing; giving a soul and identity to the character or story or environment or whatever the situation is. That's why hearing skyrim music accidentally makes you wanna install it again for the 69th time.

ART & FILM

Painting, making short funny shit movies with my old samsung s3310, acting and mimicing the voice and speaking style of my teachers and relatives, I saw all these as just a simple hobby when I was a kid. I was thinking I better put these skills to good use and create content and movies. why not? That's were I tried to learn some vfx and like all other people who had the same f@#king idea, I started watching videocopilot tutorials. Then I got familiar with 3D graphics and tried to learn almost everything about computer graphics and visual effects. The spark for most people who get into these stuff is almost always a cool vfx they saw when they were a teenager and that's what inspired them to learn 3D graphics and make their own cool effects. well... It was a little different for me. When I saw some videos of game characters in shortfilms which were ususally made with SFM (source film maker) back then, I felt like I also wanna make my own short stories with game characters about funny things related to the game. I started working with SFM (god help those people who still use it today) and I realized it's limited to some pre-made content and requires extra tweaking to achieve a desirable result. That's where I considered trying to learn blender and make my own original content. while learning blender, I discovered some special content (if you know, you know) that were mostly created by blender. That was the real spark for me. Those special 3D animations inspired me to continue learning after all my frustration with blender. These animations are actually complex and need way more skill than regular animations. So I'm still learning the average stuff to increase my skills for my ultimate ideas.
